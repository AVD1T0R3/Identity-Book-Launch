Cultural Diversity

Fun Fact:

Ethnic maps are always being redrawn by real life: in Uganda, communities like the Bakiga have migrated due to land scarcity, intermarrying with local groups like the Batooro, while South Sudanese newcomers bring fresh cultural blends—all proving that identity is less about rigid borders and more about shared schools, markets, and marriages.
