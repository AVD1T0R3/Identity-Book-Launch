Love Across Borders

Fun Fact:

The ultimate family reunion starter? A railway! A Ugandan woman once married a Pakistani doctor brought by the British to build the East African Railway, starting a sprawling family of 18 children. Their love story was later matched by their daughter, who met her husband—a Comorian-Zanzibari man—during visits to a community elder's home in Kampala.
