Colonialism and The Law

Fun Fact:

In Uganda, an abandoned baby (a foundling) can automatically become a citizen, while an ethnic group like the Banyamulenge—with deep historical roots in the same land—can still be denied citizenship today, all because of borders drawn by colonial powers over a century ago.
